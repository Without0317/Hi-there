About

I’m Khen Patra, also known as Without0317 in the music scene. I’m a high school senior from Indonesia, but my influences are more aligned with trends from English-speaking countries than local ones. Although I’m a laid-back person, my sharp resting face sometimes makes people think I’m cold or judgmental, but I’m always willing to help—just ask nicely.

Right now, I’m focused on creating music videos for my own songs while also coding and building my personal website. I have a passion for both technology and music, and I plan to pursue Computer Science in college. My skills include programming, designing, animating, playing and creating music. While I’m typically the one ensuring everything runs smoothly, I can get overly analytical when something sparks my interest.

I live by the principle that "everything must be able to be explained," and I strive to master everything I set out to do. Whether it’s coding, music, or art, I’m always aiming for excellence.

While I don’t have a long list of achievements, I’ve always been a top student during my early school years, placing first and second in exams. I’ve also made it to the city level of a science Olympiad in informatics and even landed 5th place in a city-wide blogging competition.

In the future, I aim to be a full-stack developer, seamlessly combining my love for coding with my creative passions in music, video production, and art.
