About

Hi there, I’m Khen Patra — a Bachelor Student of Informatics Engineering at ITS (Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember) with a wide range of interests and skills.


Throughout my life, I have picked up the following skills:
- English
- Coding
- Composing Music
- Photography
- Illustrating
- Problem Solving
- Logical Thinking
- Etc


I don’t believe in narrowing myself to a single role or title. Instead, I explore whatever piques my curiosity, Some might call me a “jack of all trades,” but I just call it staying curious.


I’m a fast learner who adapts easily, thinks logically, and constantly looks for ways to improve the systems and experiences around me. I enjoy breaking things down, analyzing how they work, and building better versions.


Right now, I’m focused on sharpening my skills and synthesizing new ideas by combining what I’ve learned across disciplines.


Skills

I'm proficient in Python, HTML, and CSS, and have working experience with C#, C++, and JavaScript in project-based or learning-focused contexts. I primarily use VSCode for development and Unity for game prototyping.


In design, I’m comfortable working with tools like Photoshop, Blender, Inkscape, CorelDRAW, and Krita — especially for abstract or non-human visual styles.


For music and media creation, I use FL Studio and LMMS to compose melodies and structure audio, and I edit videos with After Effects and DaVinci Resolve, often focusing on creative pacing, timing, and mood.


I’m a fast learner with strong analytical thinking and a tendency to break problems down logically. I adapt quickly to new tools and challenges, often working independently across disciplines.


My ability to synthesize concepts from different fields helps me create with both structure and originality. I'm fluent in both English and Indonesian, with ongoing experience in Japanese. Whether it's optimizing workflows, navigating creative decisions, or approaching abstract problems with clarity, I thrive in environments that reward curiosity, precision, and cross-functional thinking.
